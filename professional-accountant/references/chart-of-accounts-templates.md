# Chart of Accounts Templates

> Reference: IFRS Foundation, ACCA Financial Reporting, ICAN Study Pack,
> SYSCOHADA (OHADA zone), FASB ASC (US GAAP)

---

## Numbering Convention

Use a **4-digit account code** system (expandable to 6 digits for large entities):

| Range | Category |
|---|---|
| 1000–1999 | Assets |
| 2000–2999 | Liabilities |
| 3000–3999 | Equity |
| 4000–4999 | Revenue |
| 5000–5999 | Cost of Goods Sold / Direct Costs |
| 6000–6999 | Operating Expenses |
| 7000–7999 | Finance Income / Costs |
| 8000–8999 | Tax |
| 9000–9999 | Extraordinary / Closing entries |

## NGO / Non-Profit Chart of Accounts (Key Differences)

- Replace Revenue with **Funds Received**:
  - 4010 Grants — Donor A
  - 4020 Grants — Donor B
  - 4030 Membership Subscriptions
  - 4040 Fundraising Income
  - 4050 Restricted Funds Received
  - 4060 Unrestricted Funds Received

- Add Fund Accounting in Equity:
  - 3010 Restricted Fund — Project A
  - 3011 Restricted Fund — Project B
  - 3020 Unrestricted Fund
  - 3030 Endowment Fund

- Replace COGS with **Programme Costs**:
  - 5010 Programme Delivery — Project A
  - 5020 Programme Delivery — Project B
  - 5030 Beneficiary Support
  - 5040 Field Operations
