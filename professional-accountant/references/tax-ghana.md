---

## 1. Corporate Income Tax (CIT)

### Rates:
| Sector | Rate |
|---|---|
| General companies | 25% |
| Free Zone companies (first 10 years) | 0% |
| Free Zone companies (after 10 years) | 15% |
| Farming (cash crops, tree crops, livestock) | 0% |
| Farming (other) | 20% |
| Mining companies | 35% |
| Petroleum companies | 35% |
| Non-traditional exports | 8% |

### Filing: Annual return within **4 months** of year-end
### Quarterly instalments: March, June, September, December (provisional)

---

## 2. VAT

| Tax | Rate | Authority |
|---|---|---|
| VAT (standard) | 15% | GRA |
| National Health Insurance Levy (NHIL) | 2.5% | GRA |
| Ghana Education Trust Fund (GETFund) | 2.5% | GRA |
| COVID-19 Levy | 1% | GRA |
| **Effective rate on taxable supply** | **21%** | — |

### Registration threshold: GHS 200,000 annual turnover
### Filing: Monthly — by **last working day of following month**

### Zero-rated supplies:
- Exported goods and services
- Basic commodities: locally produced textiles, domestic accommodation
- Educational materials

### Exempt supplies:
- Financial services, insurance
- Basic food items (unprocessed): vegetables, fruits, grains, fish
- Pharmaceutical goods on the essential drugs list
- Medical services
- Educational services

---

## 3. PAYE

### Tax bands (2024):
| Annual Income (GHS) | Rate |
|---|---|
| 0 – 4,380 | 0% |
| 4,381 – 5,580 | 5% |
| 5,581 – 6,780 | 10% |
| 6,781 – 11,580 | 17.5% |
| 11,581 – 59,580 | 25% |
| Above 59,580 | 30% |

### Remittance: **By 15th of following month** to GRA
### Social Security: **13% employer + 5.5% employee** (SSNIT contribution)
   - Employer pays 13% of basic salary (not deducted from employee)
   - Employee pays 5.5% of basic salary (deducted from salary)
   - Remit to SSNIT by last day of month

## 4. Withholding Tax (Ghana)

| Payment | Resident | Non-Resident |
|---|---|---|
| Dividends | 8% | 8% |
| Interest | 8% | 8% |
| Rent | 8% | 15% |
| Royalties | 15% | 15% |
| Management fees | 20% | 20% |
| Professional fees | 20% | 20% |
| Construction contracts | 5% | 15% |

## Ghana Tax Calendar

| Deadline | Obligation |
|---|---|
| 15th of every month | PAYE remittance |
| Last day of month | SSNIT remittance |
| Last working day of month | VAT return & payment |
| Monthly | WHT remittance |
| Quarterly | Provisional CIT instalments |
| Within 4 months of year-end | Annual CIT return |
