## Core Accounting Principles

### The Foundation: Double-Entry System
```
Assets = Liabilities + Equity
Debit (Dr) = Credit (Cr) — always
```

| Account Type | Normal Balance | Increases With | Decreases With |
|-------------|----------------|----------------|----------------|
| Asset        | Debit          | Dr             | Cr             |
| Liability    | Credit         | Cr             | Dr             |
| Equity       | Credit         | Cr             | Dr             |
| Revenue      | Credit         | Cr             | Dr             |
| Expense      | Debit          | Dr             | Cr             |

### Accounting Bases
- **Cash Basis**: Record when cash moves. Micro-businesses and sole traders.
- **Accrual Basis**: Record when earned/incurred. Required for IFRS/GAAP. Mandatory for VAT-registered businesses in most jurisdictions.
- **Modified Cash**: Hybrid. Common in NGOs and government entities.

## Financial Health Ratios

**Liquidity**
- Current Ratio = Current Assets ÷ Current Liabilities (target ≥ 1.5)
- Quick Ratio = (Current Assets − Inventory) ÷ Current Liabilities (target ≥ 1.0)

**Profitability**
- Gross Profit Margin = (Revenue − COGS) ÷ Revenue × 100
- Net Profit Margin = Net Income ÷ Revenue × 100
- ROE = Net Income ÷ Shareholders' Equity

**Efficiency**
- Receivables Days = (Trade Receivables ÷ Revenue) × 365 (lower = better)
- Inventory Turnover = COGS ÷ Average Inventory (higher = better)

**Leverage**
- Debt-to-Equity = Total Debt ÷ Equity (>2.0 risky for SMEs)
- Interest Coverage = EBIT ÷ Interest Expense (target ≥ 3.0)

## Red Flags — Always Scan For These
- Revenue recorded without supporting contracts/invoices
- Expenses without receipts or approvals
- Unexplained journal entries with round numbers
- Director/owner drawings coded as business expenses
- Bank accounts not reconciled for > 30 days
- VAT collected but not remitted to tax authority
- Payroll not reconciled to actual headcount
- Intercompany balances not reconciled in group structures
- Cash float chronically short without explanation
